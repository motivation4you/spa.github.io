About LeoSPA

home / About

Spa Experience by Better is an award winning concept, based on making day spas accessible and affordable for the whole community.

We work hard to create a friendly day spa environment that is inclusive and welcoming, providing affordable luxury delivered by friendly, trained professionals.

A WARM WELCOME



You can always expect a warm welcome when you visit our spas. Our staff are chosen not only for their technical expertise but also their positive attitudes and friendly characters. If you’re a first time visitor to a day spa we’ll be delighted to give you a tour and showcase the fantastic benefits that thermal, well-being and beauty treatments can bring you.



OUR LOCATIONS



We have day spa locations in Belfast, Hertfordshire, London, and Surrey. Many of them in historic buildings such as Bethnal Green’s York Hall or the Ironmonger Row Baths in Islington. Our spas respect the historic nature of the sites, while offering modern treatments and relaxation.



Spa Experience is a product of GLL (Greenwich Leisure Limited) working in partnership with local councils to deliver a quality service that locals can afford. GLL is a charitable social enterprise for all the community, a not-for-profit organisation with a firm belief in the power of social values. GLL’s success means we’re proud to be the first leisure operator in the UK to be awarded the prestigious Social Enterprise Mark and the Big Society Award in 2011.
