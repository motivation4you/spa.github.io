SPA Membership Packages

home / Membership

Spa Experience is part of Better, one of the UK’s leading leisure providers and social enterprises.

You don’t need to be a Better member to use our day spas, but regular spa users who join can enjoy lots of benefits and discounts. Joining one of our Better membership schemes will give you access not only to our spas, but to Better centres across London – including our gyms, fitness classes and swimming – along with many other member benefits.

CHOOSE YOUR SPA MEMBERSHIP PACKAGE



BETTER HEALTH AND FITNESS UK PLUS SPA MEMBERSHIP – FOR ACCESS TO ALL OUR DAY SPAS $83.95 PER MONTH.



Membership benefits include:



  • Inclusive use of the thermal spas at all LeoSpa locations.
  • Up to 30% off selected spa treatments
  • Up to 20% off selected spa products
  • Inclusive access to the gym, fitness classes and swimming at Better Centres across Berezne
  • Inclusive personalised fitness induction and personal exercise programme
  • Racquets court access within your home borough (1 court per day)
  • Inclusive access to one 2 hour Crèche session for one child per day within your home borough


BETTER HEALTH AND FITNESS PLUS SPA – FOR ACCESS TO ONE DAY SPA FROM £49.95 (PRICE WILL VARY PER LOCATION).



Membership benefits include:



  • Inclusive use of the thermal spa at your home spa location only (excluding Ironmonger Row Baths)
  • Up to 30% off selected spa treatments
  • Up to 20% off selected spa products
  • Inclusive access to the gym, fitness classes and swimming at Better Centres across Berezne
  • Inclusive personalised fitness induction and personal exercise programme
  • Racquets court access within your home borough (one court per day)
  • Inclusive access to one two hour Crèche session for one child per week within your home borough
